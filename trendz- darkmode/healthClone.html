Causes of Sleep Apnea

Possible causes or risk factors for sleep apnea include: 1. Obesity 2. A narrow throat 3. A round head 4. Hypothyroidism 5. Excess growth due to hormones 6. Allergies 7. Deviated septum (problem with nose structure) 8. Medical conditions that congest upper airways 9. Smoking 10. Alcohol or drug abuse 11. Stroke, heart failure, or kidney failure
